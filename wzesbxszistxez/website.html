Our
Story

Our team started in the web hosting industry. After a few years, we discovered there were too many challenges to creating websites. Traditional web hosting services were complicated and expensive to manage.

Seeing a need for simplicity, the Website.com site builder was born. We wanted to offer a website building platform that requires no coding skills or design experience, with everything needed to get published online. You can simply focus on creating an amazing website that reflects your brand.

After seeing an increased need for ecommerce solutions, we developed one of the only fully-featured, free and commission-free online store builders. As our global interface grew, we expanded our editor with the ability to create multilingual websites.
